Red Oak Digital is a technology research firm that believes in supporting the development of innovative companies and projects to propel the modern world forward.

We seek to establish ourselves as an industry leader in discovering early emerging technologies. With this in mind, we see the firm as a method of improving speed and velocity in the creation of new technologies. Thesis
Layer 1 Protocols
The Race to Own the Settlement Layer

Layer 1 blockchains compete for the right to serve as the foundational settlement layer for global digital economic activity. We believe high-throughput, low-latency networks — specifically Solana and Ethereum — represent the clearest long-term opportunity in digital assets. Solana's focus on reducing transaction costs and increasing bandwidth mirrors the evolution of internet infrastructure in the 90s. We hold conviction through market cycles.

AI & Energy Infrastructure
Powering the Intelligence Revolution

The AI compute boom is a multi-decade secular trend, and the constrained resource is not models — it's physical compute and the energy to power it. We take long-term equity positions in GPU infrastructure plays and the energy companies enabling them. These are the picks and shovels of the most transformative technological shift since the internet.

Digital Assets — BTC
Bitcoin as Sovereign Reserve Asset

Bitcoin's fixed supply, decentralized security model, and global liquidity make it the most credible non-sovereign store of value ever created. Our BTC position is a long-duration macro bet on the continued erosion of fiat trust and the rise of individual monetary sovereignty. We do not trade this position.
